About Our Calories Burned Prediction Web App

Our calories burned prediction web app is designed to help users estimate the number of calories they burn during various activities. Here's what you need to know:

Purpose

Our goal is to provide a user-friendly tool that empowers individuals to make informed decisions about their fitness and health by predicting calories burned accurately.

How It Works

Users input various parameters such as activity type, duration, and intensity level into our app. Based on this data, our algorithm calculates the estimated calories burned and presents the results to the user.

Technology Stack

Our web app is built using modern technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and the Tailwind CSS framework. We also utilize Python and machine learning libraries for accurate calorie prediction.

Team

Our dedicated team of developers and fitness enthusiasts collaborated to bring this app to life. We are passionate about health and wellness and strive to make a positive impact through our work.

Future Plans

We're constantly working to improve our app and add new features. Stay tuned for upcoming updates, including enhanced activity tracking, personalized recommendations, and more!
